Dear NSS Family and Friends,

Welcome to the official website of the National Service Scheme (NSS) at Netaji Subhas University of Technology.

At NSS, our mission is to empower students through impactful community service, nurturing a sense of social responsibility, empathy, and leadership. Guided by our motto — Not Me, But You — we believe in the transformative power of selfless service and collective action to bring about meaningful change.

Whether you're a dedicated volunteer, a prospective member, or simply someone interested in our work, we invite you to explore the many initiatives and opportunities featured on our site. From social outreach to campus events, every effort is a step toward building a better tomorrow.

Join us as we continue our journey of service, learning, and growth. Together, let's foster compassion, inspire action, and create a lasting impact—on campus, in our communities, and beyond.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to your active engagement in our endeavors!

Warm regards,
The NSS NSUT Team
